About us

Budunkafunk………. A diverse group of personalities, brought together by cosmic forces to make medicinal music for the needy souls of Southern California and beyond….. conceived and born in Santa Barbara, CA, Budunkafunk as a project seeks to make music that makes people loosen up from there daily grind and move their body to the grooves of funk, soul, and little bit of jamming thrown in to bring in an extra dance vibe. With hopes to tour the west coast and beyond, Budunkafunk also has there eyes on the music festival circuit. If your looking for an all around good time with vibes of freedom, revolution and standing up for what is right, as well as just having a funky, freaky good time, Budunkafunk is a band you should be looking out for.
